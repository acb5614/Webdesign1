Museum of Science

I chose to redesign the Museum of Science identity in Graphic Design Studio II. Science and math were always my favorite subjects in school, so I was incredibly enthusastic to incorporate these concepts into my visual design. The MOS logo, is comprised of a battery, Boston, a resistor and the figurative light bulb.

The symbols are based upon scientific circuit diagrams, which appear flat and typically in a square.
